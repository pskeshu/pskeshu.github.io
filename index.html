P. S. Kesavan

Fundamental Researcher

Specializing in Cell Biology, Biophysics, Software for Microscopy, and Image Analysis

Goals

  • To solve fundamental problems that act as barriers in understanding living entities at the cellular level, spanning technological challenges in hardware, software, wetware, and conceptual frameworks.
  • To develop methods that connect imaging to insight generation processes by mimicking human scientific cognition in code, enabling the emergence of machine scientific cognition.
  • To integrate different forms of intelligence—human intuition, biological self-organization, and computational analysis—in collaborative frameworks that transform how we extract knowledge from microscopic observations.
