1. In search of

In the race to make things,
made many have we;
what use is a few,
when there is plenty.

Context matters always,
unsure are the ways;
what we have, we don't understand,
run around in circles do we.

Where do we go from here,
and where do we come from?
You're looking here and there,
yet what you seek is within you,

says the hippie.
--

2. fruitless existence

A tree starts as a seed,
grows into a plant,
twists and twirls
to find the sun.

Until one day,
the tree asks self,
why do I exist, if
I cannot even run.

Passersby listened to the worries
felt sad, and gave a shoulder to cry.
Oh giant tree, you must exist,
for you provide shade in the sun.

Unconvinced, the tree asked,
what use of I, if
fruits are not bourn by me.

3. I refuse

Despite all the trials,
tribulations and temptations
I refuse to die.
To die is to say,
there is no hope.
And to say there is no hope,
Is an insult to god.
Not to the god of the gaps,
But That that keeps the world going.
That that sparks life. The Chance.
That Encounter. That Miracle.
Of Life.
--
