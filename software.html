Software

Smart Microscopy

deepthought 2.0

Enhanced version of the microscopy experiment framework with domain model spanning theory of Observation, Perception, Biology based on the initial work of autotc and brushstrokes
Source: https://github.com/ndsystems/deepthought
Papers: arXiv bioRxiv

deepthought 1.0

Generic online experiment framework for microscopy automation based on bluesky
Source: https://github.com/ndsystems/deepthought/tree/v1.0/deepthought
Paper: bioRxiv

hard-link

Networking library for remotely connecting and controlling multiple microscopes
Source: https://github.com/ndsystems/hard-link
Paper: bioRxiv

deepthought-react

Experimental React-based web interface for microscope control
Source: https://github.com/ndsystems/deepthought-react

autotc

Digital twin of a working tissue culture facility that models experimental workflows, from frozen vials through flask seeding, media composition, cell culture monitoring, and tracking confluence levels. Provides experiment planning and tracking capabilities for cell culture operations.
Source: https://github.com/pskeshu/autotc
Background: arXiv
Adapted into deepthought/biology.py

brushstrokes

Post-modern microscope software centered around the sample, implementing perception and observation frameworks for automated microscopy. Provides domain models for perceiving objects from images.
Source: https://github.com/pskeshu/brushstrokes
Background: arXiv
Adapted into observation.py and microscopy_loop.py

Image Analysis

fai

Single camera widefield fluorescence anisotropy imaging analysis library
Source: https://github.com/pskeshu/fai
Paper: Molecular Biology of the Cell
Useful for CairnResearch optosplit II modules

anton

Agent-based image analysis and discovery framework. Illustrating how to use VLMs for the purpose of automated discovery and understanding where imaging is a basis of observation.
Source: https://github.com/pskeshu/anton
Background: arXiv
